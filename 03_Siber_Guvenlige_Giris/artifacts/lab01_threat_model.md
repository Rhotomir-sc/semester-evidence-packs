# Threat Model (STRIDE-lite)

## S — Spoofing (impersonation)
- Fake GitHub/LinkedIn profiles pretending to be me

## T — Tampering
- Malicious commits or PRs, repo file replacement

## R — Repudiation
- Lack of audit trail / unclear evidence of changes

## I — Information Disclosure
- Leaking sensitive info in screenshots/logs (usernames, IPs, emails)
- Exposing private notes or documents

## D — Denial of Service
- Account lockout, rate limits, repo abuse

## E — Elevation of Privilege
- Credential theft, token compromise, reused passwords
